About This Property

Welcome to this beautifully presented three-bedroom detached family home, set on a quiet cul-de-sac in the sought-after Greenwood Heights area. This stunning property has been lovingly maintained by its current owners and offers spacious, light-filled accommodation throughout.

The ground floor features a generous open-plan kitchen/diner with bi-fold doors opening onto the landscaped rear garden, a separate living room, and a useful utility room. Upstairs you'll find three well-proportioned bedrooms, a family bathroom, and an en-suite to the master bedroom.

The property benefits from a private driveway with parking for two vehicles, a double garage, and a mature garden to the rear. Ideally situated within walking distance of excellent local schools, shops, and transport links.

Location & Neighbourhood

Greenwood Heights is a popular residential area known for its excellent schools, green spaces, and strong community feel. The property is within walking distance of Greenwood Primary School (Ofsted Outstanding) and a short drive from the town centre.

Nearby amenities include a supermarket, doctors surgery, multiple parks, and regular bus services into the city centre. The nearest motorway junction is 4 miles away.
